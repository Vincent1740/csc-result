GPA Calculation

Grade Point Average (GPA) is calculated using the formula:

GPA = Total Grade Points ÷ Total Credit Units

CGPA Calculation

Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA) is calculated as:

CGPA = Total Grade Points for All Semesters ÷ Total Credit Units for All Semesters
